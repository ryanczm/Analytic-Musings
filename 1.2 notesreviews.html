Notes & Reviews

(All notes taken in Excel). Right now, I have attempted to follow formalized structure for note taking in excel (including idiosyncratic things like font styles, colors, and a very hierarchical style of writing out the table of contents of a book in a proper format before attempting it, with emphasis on extreme, hierarchical structure).

This is of course to facilitate learning, but things need to be very, very structured, so you know exactly where a section or subsection stands in relation to the overall structure of a book. I prefer doing notes in Excel because of how structured you can make the notes, yet contain them all very compactly in a single workbook. Of course, for effective speed, keyboard shortcuts must be used.
