Note/Disclaimer - 8th August 2022 : This used to be a post keeping track of elementary courses/textbooks to cover when I first started my learning journey 1.5 years ago. It was quite cringe so I got rid of it.

Now, my PKM systems are Notion, Obsidian & Whimsical. Notion is for task & project management (personal planning), Obsidian is my second brain (Molecular Notes). This is adopted from Robert Martin's (a really smart quant my age) posts his PKM systems. Whimsical is my own addition, which lets me track & order information sources (e.g textbooks). Notion is tabular, Obsidian & Whimsical are graph-based.

I originally kept my "second brain" in Notion, but realized the limitations, and hence moved it to Obsidian a few weeks ago. For reference, here is my Whimsical. Obsidian is for concepts, Whimsical is for sources.
