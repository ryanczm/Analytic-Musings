Stat Notes:

Last updated: 7 Jan 2020 » Stat notes (rudimentary) Wassermann » Link to notes

Progress:

First week of 2021. Dropped halfway to do ML course


Review:

Went through the first few chapters (Probability, Statistical (Bayesian) Inference). Didn't cover the remaining chapters (Statistical Decision Theory & Models) because school was starting & I was running out of time. Surprisingly heavy on notation.


Dislikes:

Unfortunately, this book was quite tough as time progressed because Wassermann throws notation and theorems at you with little explanation. For example, when talking about common continuous distributions, the PDF & CDF of Gamma & Beta distributions is shown, with no explanation at all what they were used for or their intuition, and thus I had to search online for more answers on their meaning. After a while I realized the stats were getting too advanced so I dropped it halfway and went to do some ML courses because of time constraints..


To do:

Probably find another better-explained statistics textbook to go through next time. >:(
